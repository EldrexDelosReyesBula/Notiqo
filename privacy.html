The main focus of our Privacy Policy is to ensure that your personal data is never at risk. Instead of storing personal identifiers, we focus on storing minimal, text-based data that is purely for functionality. Users can trust that we are transparent about our practices and are dedicated to maintaining their privacy. This approach is especially relevant in today’s environment where privacy concerns are paramount. ChronoCraft Calendar by LanDecs is committed to ensuring that no sensitive or personal data is stored externally or used for any nefarious purpose, such as marketing or profiling.

Local Storage

We have taken the extra step of ensuring that all data remains strictly confined to your device. This means that ChronoCraft Calendar by LanDecs does not sync your data to the cloud, nor does it transmit it to any external servers. Local storage is essential in providing full control over your data. It gives users a sense of security, as they can trust that their inputted data is not stored on a central server that could potentially be compromised. This ensures that no unauthorized entities can access your data.

Local storage also gives users the benefit of not needing an internet connection to access their data. The data is readily available whenever the app is used, and no external syncing or internet connectivity is required. This also reduces potential risks related to cloud data storage breaches, as no data is being transmitted across the web.
